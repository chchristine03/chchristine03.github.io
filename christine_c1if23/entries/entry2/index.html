Today, I Appreciate... "PEACE FROM THE CHAOS"

From New York City to Boston

September 16, 2023

@Norman B. Leventhal Park
Boston, Massachusetts

We Chase What We Don't Have.
At Least I Do.

I went on a short trip from New York City to Boston with my friends over the weekend.
Exploring parts of the city felt like a reward.
I am surrounded by the tall buildings, yet I don't feel too small.
I wish I could keep following the river until I am home.

A part of me wanted this to be my new home instead of NYC.
Because the environment seemed calmer, I was able to mindlessly walk. I was emptying my thoughts, yet I felt so filled by the wide view of nature.

i chase peace.

I feel grateful for today
